## What this is

Parking Observatory is a free, community-run tool for documenting long-term parked vehicles in the Karl-Kunger Kiez neighbourhood, Berlin. It exists to give residents a shared, factual picture of the local parking situation. It is not affiliated with any government authority, and it does not report vehicles to the police, city, or any enforcement body.

## Acceptable use

By using this app, you agree to:
- Report only vehicles you have genuinely observed, in good faith and to the best of your knowledge
- Not use the app to harass, target, or make false reports about specific individuals
- Not attempt to access moderator functions without authorisation
- Not use the app to collect data for any purpose other than viewing community parking information

We reserve the right to remove reports, block access, or restrict use of the app in cases of misuse.

## Accuracy of information

Reports are submitted by community members and are not independently verified except where marked. We do not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of any information on this app. A vehicle's presence or absence on the map should not be relied upon for legal, enforcement, or safety decisions.

## Photo reporting

If you report a vehicle by photo, the photo is used only to extract the vehicle's location and licence plate, and is not stored after that process completes. By submitting a photo, you confirm you took it yourself and that doing so did not put you or anyone else at risk.
